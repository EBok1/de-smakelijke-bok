## Ingredients

- 4 large ripe tomatoes
- 2 balls fresh mozzarella (125g each)
- Fresh basil leaves
- 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
- Optional: balsamic glaze

## Instructions

1. **Slice the tomatoes** into rounds about 1 cm thick.

2. **Slice the mozzarella** into equally thick rounds.

3. **Arrange the slices** alternately on a large plate, slightly overlapping.

4. **Scatter the basil** over the top.

5. **Drizzle with olive oil** and season with salt and pepper.

6. **Serve immediately**, optionally with a drizzle of balsamic glaze.

## Tips

- Use tomatoes at room temperature for the best flavor.
- Buy the freshest mozzarella you can find - it really makes a difference!
- Add some pine nuts for extra crunch.
