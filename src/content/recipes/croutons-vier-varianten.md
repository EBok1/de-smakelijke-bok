## Ingredients

### Base
- 2 slices of bread (day-old bread works great)
- 4 tablespoons olive oil
- Knob of butter (optional)
- Salt and pepper

### Variation 1: Classic with herbs
- Base ingredients
- 2 teaspoons dried herbs (e.g. Italian herbs)

### Variation 2: Cheese-onion
- Base ingredients
- 20 grams grated cheese
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- 1 clove garlic (finely sliced)

### Variation 3: Garlic-rosemary
- Base ingredients
- 2 sprigs fresh rosemary
- 1 clove garlic (finely sliced)

### Variation 4: Pesto
- Base ingredients
- 4 tablespoons pesto

## Equipment

- Air fryer
- Cutting board and knife
- Bowl

## Instructions

1. **Cut the bread**: Cut the bread into small cubes of about 1-2 cm.

2. **Preheat the air fryer**: Let the air fryer preheat for 5 minutes at 180Â°C (350Â°F).

3. **Mix**: Put the bread cubes in a bowl and mix with the olive oil, butter and the ingredients from your chosen variation.

4. **Fry**: Put everything in the air fryer and fry for 15 minutes until golden brown and crispy. Shake or stir halfway through.

5. **Serve**: Delicious in soup, through a salad or just as a snack.
